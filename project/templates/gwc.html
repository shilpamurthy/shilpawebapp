What we are cooking...

The girls are working on a web application that will help high school students prioritize and schedule academics, physical and mental strength, extra curricular activities, stress and sleep. The girls already reseached many existing web and mobile applications, noting down the functionalities and short-comings of each application. They have further narrowed down the specific functionalities that they would like to incorporate in their application. They are now learning Python, HTML and CSS so that they can implement this.
